Genre: Mystery-drama

Years: 2004-2010

Episodes: 121
Country of Origin: United States

Introduction

Lost is a mystery–drama television series that follows the survivors of a plane crash stranded on a mysterious tropical island.
As they struggle to stay alive, they uncover secrets about the island — and about themselves — leading to unexpected twists,
supernatural elements, and deep emotional connections.
